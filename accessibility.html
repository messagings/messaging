Accessibility

Built for everyone.

Our commitment to making Messaging usable by everyone, everywhere.

Messaging is built to be usable by everyone. We believe accessibility is not a feature — it's a requirement. This page outlines our commitments, current status, and how to report issues.

Our Commitment

We target compliance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA across all surfaces of Messaging. We conduct accessibility reviews during design and development, and we take reports of accessibility issues seriously.

Screen Reader Support

All interactive elements are properly labelled for screen readers. We test with VoiceOver (iOS/macOS) and TalkBack (Android) regularly.

Keyboard Navigation

The full Messaging web interface is navigable by keyboard. Focus states are visible and logical in tab order.

Colour Contrast

All text meets or exceeds the WCAG AA contrast ratio. We do not rely on colour alone to convey information.

Text Sizing

The interface responds correctly to system font size preferences and browser text scaling without breaking layouts.
